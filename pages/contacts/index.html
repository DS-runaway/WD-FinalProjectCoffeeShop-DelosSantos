Every cup tells a story.

We are a coffee shop chain located within the Philippines and founded by Manuel Villar Jr. in the year 2014. Our goal is to improve the Philippine coffee experience for all coffee lovers and establish a new coffee culture focused on specialty single-origin coffees and an elegant, garden-inspired ambiance.

The Coffee Project is your go-to cafe to relax, recharge, and truly “espresso” yourself. So if you have the time, swing by any Coffee Project branch near you for a cup that best personifies you. Snap a pic, share it, and don't forget to tag us! We can't wait to see your coffee moments with us!
